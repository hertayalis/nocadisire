bobby smallwood shooting and bobby smallwood legacy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

It is with immense sadness that we share the news of a devastating tragedy that struck Legacy Good Samaritan Medical Center. On a fateful Saturday morning, the hospital became the site of a shocking shooting incident that claimed the life of one security officer and left the entire community in mourning. In this article, we will delve into the events surrounding the Bobby Smallwood shooting, the investigation that followed, and the legacy he leaves behind as one of the dedicated safety and security officers.

The Shooting Incident

The shooting incident unfolded on the fifth floor of the Irving Center, near the hospital's birthing unit, at approximately 11 a.m. on that ill-fated day. A hail of gunfire erupted, sending shockwaves through the hospital and surrounding community. Officers from the Portland Police Bureau (PPB) responded swiftly, treating the situation as a potential active assailant scenario. Despite limited information at the time, their priority was to protect innocent lives.

Regrettably, Bobby Smallwood, a devoted safety and security officer at Legacy Good Samaritan, lost his life while heroically safeguarding patients and fellow staff members. Another staff member was injured but is now in stable condition. Fortunately, no patients suffered physical harm during the incident. The suspect was later neutralized by a police officer in Gresham, putting an end to the immediate threat.

Mourning a Hero

The news of Bobby Smallwood's passing has sent shockwaves of grief throughout Legacy Health and the broader community. Kathryn Correia, president and CEO of Legacy Health, expressed profound sadness and offered unwavering support to Bobby's loved ones, patients in their care, and the entire staff at Legacy Good Samaritan during this difficult time.

Bobby Smallwood's legacy will forever be remembered as that of a brave and selfless individual who put others before himself. His commitment to ensuring the safety and well-being of patients and employees made him an invaluable asset to the hospital.

Impact on Legacy Good Samaritan Medical Center

In the wake of the tragedy, Legacy Good Samaritan Medical Center implemented Code Silver, initiating a lockdown to ensure the safety of everyone on the premises. Employees were required to use their badges for entry, and certain departments, such as the Emergency Department and the Family Birth Center, were placed on divert status. Despite the somber circumstances, the hospital aimed to maintain normal operations to continue providing essential healthcare services.

Supporting the Community

Recognizing the need for support during this challenging time, Legacy Good Samaritan Medical Center provided counseling services to its employees. In addition, a staging area was set up in the parking lot of the 1120 N.W. 20th Ave. building to offer assistance and comfort to the families of both employees and patients.

To ensure timely updates, Legacy Health established dedicated phone lines for employees and families of patients. By calling the Legacy Health Employee Phone Line at 503-225-6242 or the Patient Family Phone Line at 503-225-6353, individuals could receive the latest information related to the situation.

Ongoing Investigation

As the incident remains an active and ongoing crime investigation, all media inquiries have been directed to the Portland Police Bureau. Law enforcement authorities are diligently working to understand the motive behind the shooting and any factors that may have contributed to the tragic event.

Conclusion

The Bobby Smallwood shooting at Legacy Good Samaritan Medical Center has left an indelible mark on the hearts of many. Bobby Smallwood's bravery and sacrifice will be remembered as a testament to the dedication of those who protect and care for others. As the community mourns the loss of a hero, it also stands united in support, seeking solace and healing during this time of sorrow.
