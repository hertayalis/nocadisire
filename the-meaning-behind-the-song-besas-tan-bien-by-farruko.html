The Meaning Behind The Song: Besas Tan Bien by Farruko

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

As someone who frequently listens to music, I have come across countless songs that have left a lasting impression on me. One such song that has captivated me is “Besas Tan Bien” by Farruko. I first heard this song on a car ride with my friends, and since then, it has become one of my favorites.

“Besas Tan Bien” translates to “You Kiss So Well” in English, and the lyrics explore the passion and intensity of an unforgettable kiss. The song begins with the line “Six million ways to die, choose one,” which sets the tone for an enticing and provocative experience.

Farruko’s vocals in this song are filled with desire and longing. He sings about being enamored by the kisses of his partner, expressing that her mouth has taken him captive. The lyrics describe the pleasure of her kisses, comparing them to the sweet taste of strawberries.

The verses delve deeper into the intoxication of their connection. Farruko mentions the skill and finesse with which she kisses him, stating that her touch mesmerizes his lips. He vividly paints a picture of their passionate encounters, igniting a fire within himself and feeling the flutter of butterflies in his stomach.

The pre-chorus adds a playful element to the song, with Farruko singing “Whine up whine up, you’re mi girla” and expressing his adoration for his partner. The chorus itself is catchy and infectious, emphasizing the indulgence and satisfaction he finds in her kisses.

Throughout the song, Farruko’s lyrics convey a sense of urgency and excitement, as he yearns to explore more of her secrets. He admits to being infatuated and anxious to experience her kisses again. The intensity of his emotions is palpable and adds to the allure of the song.

“Besas Tan Bien” was released on May 23, 2013, as part of Farruko’s album “Imperio Nazza: Farruko Edition.” The song was produced by Musicólogo and Menes and written by Farruko and Benny Benni. The music video, directed by David Aponte, further enhances the sensuality portrayed in the lyrics.

In conclusion, “Besas Tan Bien” is a song that celebrates the power of a breathtaking kiss. Its lyrics explore the ecstasy and intense connection formed in such an intimate moment. Farruko’s passionate vocals and the catchy chorus make this song a true standout. If you’re looking for a track that will ignite your senses and make you yearn for that perfect kiss, give “Besas Tan Bien” a listen. You won’t be disappointed.
