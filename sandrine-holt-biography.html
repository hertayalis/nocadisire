Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Who is Sandrine Holt?

Sandrine Holt is a British-Canadian actress and model. She is mostly known for being in Rapa Nui (1994), Dance Me Outside (1994), and Pocahontas: The Legend (1995).

In 2020, she was seen in eight episodes of a TV series, The Expanse as Oksana.

Sandrine Holt- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born as Sandrine Ho on 19 Nov 1972 in London, England, UK. The name of her father is Man Shun and her mother’s name is Christiane née Nicolette.

Sandrine’s ancestry is French-Asian and has a younger sister Adrianne Ho (model and designer).

Education

Talking about her academics, she has attended St. Joseph’s Morrow Park Catholic Secondary School and the University of London.

From there, she received two degrees in B.Sc Physics and Applied Mathematics and M.Sc in Computer Science.

Sandrine Holt- Professional Life, Career

Sandrine Holt was first seen in a TV series titled Friday the 13th: The Series in 1989. Then she has seen in movies like Black Robe, Gunslinger’s Revenge, Century Hotel, Sam’s Lake, and more.

Her recent films are Chinese Puzzle (2015) as Ju, Terminator Genisys (2015) as a detective, and Sorry for Your Loss (2017) as Tracey.

But her fame is associated with TV shows like New York Undercover (1997), Las Vegas (2005), Burn Up (2008), Fear the Walking Dead (2015), and more.
