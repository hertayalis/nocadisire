Biography:

Kim Matula was born on August 23, 1988 in Fort Worth, Texas. After high school, she attended University of Texas at Arlington to study acting. She had to quit when she received television offers. In 2006, she made her screen debut in the movie Savage Spirit. Her next role came 2 years later with the TV movie Queen Sized.

After landing a guest role on How I Met Your Mother, she got a main role on the soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful. She played the role of Hope Logan from 2010 to 2014. She returned as a guest in 2015 and 2016. She has also appeared in the films Dawn Patrol and Maybe Someday. As of now, she stars on the TV series UnReal as part of the main cast.
