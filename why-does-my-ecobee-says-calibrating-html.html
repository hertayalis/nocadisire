What is the accuracy of the ecobee temperature?

What is the accuracy of the temperature sensor in the ecobee smart thermostat?

The ecobee temperature sensor has an accuracy of +/- 1.0 degree Fahrenheit and is set to send data once every 15 seconds. It will take the previous two readings and use them to calculate the current reading by averaging them.
