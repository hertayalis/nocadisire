Sheck Wes- Professional Life, Careers

Sheck Wes signed the contract with Kanye West’s Good Music and Travis Scott’s Cactus Jack Records label on February 2, 2018. He appeared as a guest on the third album, Astroworld on the song”No Bystanders” along with rapper Juice Wrld.

His singles “Losing My Mind” and “Sadio Mane” were released on July 18, 2019. On the same day, he made appearances on the song “MAYDAY” by Chase B and Young Thug.
