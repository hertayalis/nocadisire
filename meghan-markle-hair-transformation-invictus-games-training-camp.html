Meghan Markle's Hair Colorist Reveals How She Transformed the Royal's Hair Ahead of Canada Trip

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 13, 2024

What better way to liven up the dreary winter than by putting a fresh new spin on your hair? It seems Duchess Meghan thought it was the perfect time for a touch-up, and we just got all the details on her transformed ’do, thanks to a new Instagram post from her hair color specialist, Kadi Lee.

The Duchess of Sussex wanted something rich and warm for her recent trip to Canada with husband Prince Harry. So she went for a chocolate chestnut brunette color, courtesy of Highbrow Hippie in California (run by Lee and Myka Harris). Previously, the royal’s hair was a darker chocolate brown; this new hue has slight auburn undertones.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

“This mixture of red and golden undertones makes a dynamic shade of brunette that not only gives skin the healthiest glow, especially during the colder months of winter, it also provides the hair with a next-level high-power shine,” Lee wrote on Instagram.

Andrew Chin//Getty Images

Meghan sported the color while attending the 2025 Invictus Games “One Year to Go” training camp with her hubby. Founded by Harry in 2014, the games are an international multisport event for wounded, injured, and sick armed forces members, whether currently serving or veterans. There have been six Invictus Games so far. Next year’s first-ever winter games will take place in Vancouver and Whistler, in British Columbia.

On last weekend’s trip, the royals were spotted at the Whistler Sliding Centre, where they learned more about the sport of skeleton—one of the new winter sports coming to the games in 2025. It was there that Meghan unveiled her striking new locks. Not only was the color quite rich, the duchess was also rocking a bouncy blowout, courtesy of Highbrow Hippie hairstylist Hector (@hectorsworldd on Instagram).
