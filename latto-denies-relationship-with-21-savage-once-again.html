Latto Denies Relationship With 21 Savage Once Again

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 17, 2024

After years of speculations, Latto continues to deny rumors that her and 21 Savage are a couple.

Yesterday (Feb. 26), the “Lottery” rapper posted a TikTok video showing herself cooking a “Soul Food Sunday” meal and many of the commenters assumed that Latto must have prepared the food for 21 Savage. One user directly asked, “Did she actually say she’s with 21 [Savage]??” Latto responded, “No, I’ve said I’m not plenty [of] times.”

21 Savage also recently addressed the rumor during a conversation on Clubhouse last December. “I’ve been hot for a long time. I don’t do all the antics and s**t. I don’t got a celebrity girlfriend," the "Spin Bout U" hitmaker said. "I’m not fittna be out everywhere. I’m not dropping music every other week. I’m not going on [Instagram] Live talking about n**gas every other week.”

Although both Atlanta-bred rappers have said their piece, the internet refuses to believe that there is no romantic connection between the two, especially when a photo of Latto apparently having the word “Sheyaa” tattooed behind her ear circulated the internet recently. Sheyaa Bin Abraham-Joseph is 21 Savage’s birth name. The duo have collaborated on two singles including 2020 "Pull Up" from Latto's debut album, Queen of da Souf and 2022 "Wheelie."
