Taylor Swift Is Debuting 4 Unreleased Songs at Midnight

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 25, 2024

More Taylor music is coming!

Taylor Swift just announced she will be releasing four new songs tonight.

"In celebration of The Eras Tour I'm releasing 4 previously unreleased songs tonight at midnight," the singer wrote on her Instagram Story.

The tracks are "Eyes Open (Taylor's Version)," "Safe & Sound" featuring Joy Williams and John Paul White (Taylor's Version), "If This Was a Movie" (Taylor's Version), and "All of the Girls You Loved Before."

Taylor Swift

The drop comes ahead of Swift's national tour, which starts tomorrow, March 17 and ends on August 9.

The Eras Tour will be Swift's sixth headlining concert tour and follows the release of her album Midnights, which broke industry records immediately upon its release last October 21. The album took the top 10 spots on Billboard's Hot 100 and was streamed 185 million times on its release day alone, per CNBC.

"We lie awake in love and in fear, in turmoil and in tears. We stare at walls and drink until they speak back. We twist in our self-made cages and pray that we aren’t - right this minute - about to make some fateful life-altering mistake," Swift wrote about the album in a post announcing its release. "This is a collection of music written in the middle of the night, a journey through terror and sweet dreams. The floors we pace and the demons we face. For all of us who have tossed and turned and decided to keep the lanterns lit and go searching - hoping that just maybe, when the clock strikes twelve … we’ll meet ourselves."

Because Swift did not tour her studio albums Lover, Folklore, and Evermore due to the COVID-19 pandemic, she will likely performs songs from all four of her recent albums during her Eras Tour.
