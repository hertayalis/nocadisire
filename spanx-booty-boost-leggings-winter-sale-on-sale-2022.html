Save on Booty Boost Leggings and More

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 24, 2024

The (unofficial) verdict is in: Celebrities and fashion editors have crowned one particular pair of leggings among the best of 2021, and that will undoubtedly still ring true in 2022.

Spanx's Booty Boost Leggings are basically as famous as the celebs who repeatedly wear them. Jennifer Garner is one avid fan who steps out for her morning walks, grocery runs, and coffee dates in various iterations of the Spanx leggings. Kourtney Kardashian and Reese Witherspoon also wear their Booty Boosts for their workouts and more. And yes, they do exactly what the name promises: boost your butt for the best behind ever. When I wear mine (for everything), it looks like I've done 100 squats a day for a year — in short, it looks great.

Whenever the leggings go on sale (which is rare, by the way!), they sell out fast. Black Friday and Cyber Monday are among the few days of the year when the Booty Boosts' $98 price tag gets slashed. But, with Spanx's the end-of-season sale happening right now, the best-selling workout bottoms are the cheapest we've ever seen, like, ever. It's all thanks to the brand's sale-on-sale event, which is offering an extra 30 percent off markdowns with the code SALE at checkout.
