Who is Godfrey the comedian?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 20, 2024

COMEDIAN and actor, Godfrey, has made quite the name for himself on stage.

Tickets to see him perform are currently on sale for fans in need of a laugh.

Godfrey C. Danchimah, Jr., 52, was born in Lincoln, Nebraska in 1969 to Nigerian parents.

He grew up in Chicago and played football at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Early on, Godfrey worked as a warm-up comedian for The Cosby Show.

He has starred in the TV series like Celebrity Paranormal Project, The Mr. Men Show, The Smoking Gun Presents: World's Dumbest, Upload with Shaquille O'Neal, Comics Unleashed, Bullseye, Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, and more.

He was also featured in movies like Original Gangstas, Chain Reaction, 30 Years to Life, Zoolander, Johnson Family Vacation, Soul Plane, Stand Up, Careless, A Dennis the Menace Christmas, and more.

He is reportedly a regular performer at the comedy club Comedy Cellar in New York City.

Godfrey hosts his own podcast called, In Godfrey We Trust, and has his own YouTube channel with 33.8k subscribers.

What is Godfrey's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Godfrey's worth is estimated to be nearly $3million.

The comedian has 639k followers on Instagram.

Will Godfrey be on tour in 2022?

2022 tickets to see Godfrey are currently on sale via TicketMaster and SeatGeek.

He is set to visit some of the following venues:

  • Addison Improv in Texas (March 18-March 20)
  • Funny Bone in Virginia Beach (March 25-March 27)
  • Improv Comedy Theather in Tampa, Florida (April 22-April 24)
  • Ontario Improv in Canada (April 28-May 1)
  • West Nyack Levity Live in New York (May 26-May 29)

Godfrey has already promoted the first two venues on his Instagram bio.
