The second series of the hit dark comedy follows Fleabag as she falls in love with a Priest while dealing with her dysfunctional family and legal troubles, and the cast have been critically lauded for their stellar performances.     Sian absolutely kills it as Fleabag's high strung older sister, Claire. In the show, Claire is a high flying businesswoman who still feels overshadowed by her witty younger sister. In season two, she is struggling with her marriage after having suffered a miscarriage. Speaking about her friendship with Phoebe, she told the : "I had just got some terrible news and I messaged Phoebe and she was like, 'Come and meet me, right now'. I think you just needed a hand, actually!'" Sian has also played Martha Crawley in ITV's adaptation of Vanity Fair.
